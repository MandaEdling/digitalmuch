Who I Am

Amanda Edlling

I am the co-founder and CEO of the tech startup Emrys Inc, a platform that manages the services and open-source tools Data Scientists use to model, visualize, and work with data. I have worked as a senior partner at Archethought LLC, managing development teams and working with clients to help guide and manage their projects. I use my technical skill set as well as artistic and design background to help shape client engagements, product vision, and customer journey while also developing software and IoT products.

I have 7 years experience in UX design and frontend design and 9 years working with startups in a variety of fields from games, media, television and IoT including one startup acquired internationally. I have consulted and worked with Nagravision, HP, DARPA and the Navy.



In my spare time I tinker around with game scripts, create concept art, watch too much Scifi and write speculative fiction.
